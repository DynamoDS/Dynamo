# Disabled Projects

## IronPython

### Projects
1. DSIronPython
2. IronPythonExtension
3. IronPythonTests

### Reason
Python2 and by extension IronPython2 has reached end of life and has many security issues

### Resolution
IronPython has been deactivated and replaced with Python.NET (pythonnet)

### LibraryViewExtension

### Projects
1. LibraryViewExtension
2. ViewExtensionLibraryTests

### Reason
LibraryViewExtension is based on the 3d party package CEF which causes incompatibility issues with integrators

### Resolution
LibraryViewExtension has been deactivated and replaced with LibraryViewExtensionMSBrowser
